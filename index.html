A design and development agency and learning group for students at the University of Kansas

We're always welcoming new members

We want to empower students with the knowledge and skills to build their own ideas and to become more valuable to companies who recognize the growing importance of design and technology. Being a member of Coral is a great way to supplement your classroom education, collaborate with great people, and work on projects for your portfolio. Students of any major or experience level are welcome; you'll learn with us! We have lessons and worknights every Thursday evening.
